Best Dog Breeds for Beginners

Are you thinking about getting a dog? If you're a first-time dog owner, you may want to consider getting a breed that's easy to care for and easy to train. Here are some of the best dog breeds for beginners:

  • Shih Tzu
  • Yorky
  • Poodle
  • Beagle
  • Bulldog
  • Pomeranian

Shih Tzu

The Shih Tzu is a small dog breed that's known for its friendly and outgoing personality. They're great with kids and other pets, and they're easy to train. Shih Tzus are also hypoallergenic, which means they're a good choice for people with allergies.

Yorky
